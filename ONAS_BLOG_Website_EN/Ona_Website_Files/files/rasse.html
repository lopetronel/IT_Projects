Important Note!

An Apso Lhasa needs good health and a balanced diet!

Because of the short nose, the Lhasa Apso may be restricted by the brachycephalic syndrome.

Good to know:

  • Lhasas were originally temple dogs
  • They are of Tibetan origin
  • Monks preferred to keep this breed
  • Prominent protective instinct
  • The perfect family dog
  • Does not tolerate too warm climate
  • Lovable and exceedingly loyal
  • Does not need much exercise
  • Wikipedia link for more info!

Lhasa Breeds

Longhair

Longhaired Lhasas are very noble dogs that require a lot of coat care

If you do not brush and wash them several times a week it can quickly come to hair tangles which can make walking difficult.
