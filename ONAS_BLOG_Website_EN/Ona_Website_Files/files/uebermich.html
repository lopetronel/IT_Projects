Ona Belfiglio

Hello everyone, I am Ona Belfiglio, a purebred Apso Lhasa who has been a member of the Belfiglio/Lopetrone family for several years.

When I was picked up from the farm of the Rebholz family by my future master, I was not even 2 years old. I was quite suspicious of them at the start, however, I have already felt very comfortable after a good month.

I was already very early house-trained and how to properly behave. However, the only problem they have with me is that I start barking every time I hear the doorbell. I just want to defend my beautiful territory!
